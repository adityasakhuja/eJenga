How It Works

Earn rent from the tenants in your invested properties as well as capital gains on the rise in property prices

Investing in a Property


Invest in properties online and filter according to your preferred investment theme. They are hand-picked by Kenya’s top Real Estate Professionals. We take care of the tedious parts; like solicitors and mortgage costs through a one-time fee

Earn Quarterly Rental Income*


Each quarterly income weighted by your proportion of the property that you own. A percentage (+ VAT) of the rent is used for letting, advertising & maintaining the property. The investor portal can be used to monitor your investment with valuations occurring at each quarter

Sell your Investment*


Offer your investments to other investors at your chosen price through our online ‘re-sale’ marketplace**. Flexibility is offered on the amount of your holdings you wish to sell. Alternatively, investors are given the opportunity to sell their holdings at market price every 3 years at no cost

*Your investment value can go up or down. Historical performance is not conclusive of future results
**Subject to buy/sell market liquidity
